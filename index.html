Features

Everything Your Restaurant Needs

A complete toolkit built for the modern food business — from a single café to a chain of restaurants.

Fast Billing System

Generate GST-compliant bills in seconds. Support for KOT, split bills, discounts, and multiple payment modes.

Table Management

Visual floor plan to manage dine-in orders, table status, and transfers with a single tap.

Inventory Management

Track raw materials, get low-stock alerts, and reduce wastage with real-time stock monitoring.

Kitchen Order Display

Instantly send orders to the kitchen display. Reduce miscommunication and speed up food delivery.

Sales Reports & Analytics

Daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports. Identify best-selling items, peak hours, and revenue trends.

GST Billing

Auto-calculate SGST, CGST, IGST. Generate GST-ready invoices and export for filing with ease.

Online & Offline Mode

Keep billing going even without internet. Data syncs automatically once you're back online.

Multi-Device Support

Run on tablets, PCs, and phones simultaneously. Perfect for busy multi-station restaurants.

Staff Management

Set roles and permissions for staff. Track shift-wise sales and manage waiter/captain assignment.
